Specific instructions for input values

    SOLAR

Efficiency of typical solar panels
Solar panels = 13% to 23 % & high performance solar panels = upto 34%

    WIND

Power coefficient (Cp) values of typical wind turbines
Horizontal axis wind turbine = 0.2 to 0.45 & Vertical axis wind turbine = 0.1 to 0.2

    TIDE

Power coefficient (Cp) values of typical tidal current turbines
Horizontal axis tidal turbine = 0.2 to 0.45 & Vertical axis tidal turbine = 0.1 to 0.2

    WAVE

Power take off (PTO) efficiency values of typical wave energy devices
Oscillating water cloumn (OWC) = 11% to 31% , Overtopping devices (OTD) = 18% to 27% & Others = 4% to 45%

    OTHERS

Carbon Intensity (CI) values in (gCO2/kWh) for various countries
